Issue 63 reconciliation prototype

Keep the terminal true when adapters add work

Question: do CLI and Dagger preserve role-specific Pack ingress, pre-core failure, request rejection, Compilation Reports, disclosure, staging, delivery, and watch currentness without flattening them? Under the no-external-v1 premise, issue 63 supersedes the unreleased issue-56 prototype in place.

Verdict under test

  • Pre-core adapter failure has an operation envelope but no Compilation Terminal.
  • Archive Content Identity expectation never leaks onto Closure Export ingress.
  • Request rejection has no Compilation Report or Compilation Identity.
  • A report owns one Result or Operation Outcome.
  • Canonical evidence is capability-gated and serializes only its trace and evidence table.
  • First-party font scans warn and omit invalid or unreadable candidates outside the core Creation Report.
  • Dagger staging and CLI delivery cannot rewrite the semantic result.
  • Superseded subscription generations retire exactly through one ordered unique collection, even during active attempts.
  • Watch dirtiness stales the existing publication and retained success immediately.
