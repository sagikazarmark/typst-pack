Verdict under test

Two layers, one effective limit

Pack Format Epoch 2 keeps generous immutable representability ceilings. Each core operation requires explicit caller-supplied limits. Target adapters own documented defaults, and callers may tighten them. The effective limit is always the stricter value; policy exhaustion leaves Pack validity unknown.

Layer 1 Format safety ceiling Universal and epoch-bound. Crossing it makes a representation invalid.
Layer 2 Operation budget Explicit target policy. Crossing it produces a rejection or resource outcome without judging universal validity.
